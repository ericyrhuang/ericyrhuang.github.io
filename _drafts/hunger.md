I've never experienced poverty or hunger.

In CBD kids begging for food. 

Giving to homeless people on the streets

I remember when I first came I was torn when I had kfc and i had gave to a kid earlier but not an old man


In cbd one of the hawkers was genuniely desperate. 

He wasn't talking about yumyum anymore, he was talking about starvation. He said he hadn't eaten in 2 days. 
When we walked into Naivas, he ran to grab bread and rice. That's all that he wanted or cared about. Stuff to just survive for another few days.

The moment that struck me was when the store owner agreeded to lower the price to 600 shillings because of him. I doubt if he was less desperate they would be willing to do this.
