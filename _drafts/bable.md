Silver bars had a word on one side and the same word but in a different language on the other. 
The magic of the bars derive from capturing some meaning that is lost in translating between the two sides. In other words, inaccuracies in translations that powered the silver match-pairs were all, technically, puns! The indescribable 

I love this gimmick. The countless times I've tried translating and explaining something in Mandarin to English and vice-versa only for it to fall flat as I struggle for the right words
It turns the feeling of frustration into power, because at the end of the day that is in some ways what it is. The inability to translate perfectly between languages is an edge that we hold over Monolinguals. It represents a fundementally unique perspective that those privy to the other language have access to and there is some inherit power here.
